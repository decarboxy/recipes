  * 8 Oz. cooked egg noodles
  * 1 can cream of chicken soup
  * 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  * 1/2 cup milk
  * 1 bag shredded cheese
  * 3-4 chicken breasts
  * 2-3 tbsp butter/margarine - melted
  * ritz cracker crumbs
 

  - Cube chicken breasts and brown till cooked through
  - combine butter and ritz cracker crumbs
  - mix all ingredients except cracker/butter mix and place in a greased casserole dish
  - top with cracker crumbs
  - Bake at 350 for roughly 20 minutes until cracker crumbs are brown and casserole is bubbling
