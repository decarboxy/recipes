## Salad

* 1 Tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
* 1-2 lb medium shrimp, peeled and diced
* 3/4 cup cucumber, peeled, seeded and cut into 1/4" cubes
* 2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
* 1 tbsp fresh chives
* 2 Avocados
* Salt
* 4 cups mixed greens

## Dressing

* Grated zest of 1 lemon
* 3 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
* 3 tbsp olive oil
* 3 tbsp mayo
* salt and pepper

## Directions

In a large sauce pan, boil 4-5 cups of water with old bay seasoning, add shrimp, lower heat and simmer for 1 minutes.  Turn off heat and rest for 3 minutes, drain and let cool.

Cut cooled shrimp into 1/2" chunks, place in bowl with cucumber, dill, and chives.

In small bowl, mix lemon zest, lemon juice and oil, whisk in mayo till creamy.  Season with salt and pepper and reserve 1 tbsp dressing for greens.  Refrigerate till ready to serve.

Serve on top of halved avocado, toss greens with remaining dressing.
