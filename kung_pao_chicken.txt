   * 2 lbs. boneless chicken
Marinade:
   * 1/4 cup white wine
   * 1/4 cup soy sauce
   * 1/4 cup sesame oil 
   * 2 tbsp cornstarch/water mixture
Sauce:
   * 3-4 green onions
   * 2-3 cloves of garlic
   * 1/2 cup brown sugar
   * 1/4 cup white vinegar
   * 1/4 cup white wine
   * 1/4 cup soy sauce
   * 1/4 cup sesame oil
   * 2 tbsp cornstarch/water
   * 4 oz. Sriracha
   * 1 8 oz. can water chestnuts
   * crushed peanuts

Marinade:
   - Combine white wine, soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch.  
   - Mix together.  
   - Place chicken in bowl and work into marinade.  
   - Place in fridge for 30 min.
Sauce:
   - Combine white wine, soy sauce, sesame oil, cornstarch.
   - Mix together and add green onion, garlic, water chestnuts, and peanuts.
   - Heat sauce in sauce pan until aromatic and sauce thickens.  Add more 1:1 mixture cornstarch/water if needed.
   - Remove chicken from marinade and saute in separate skillet.  Add sauce to chicken and sprinkle peanuts on top.
