====== Three-bean chili ======

10 servings, 6 points per serving

  * 1 large minced onion
  * 1 clove minced garlic
  * 30 oz canned black beans rinsed and drained
  * 30 oz canned kidney beans rinsed and drained
  * 30 oz canned pinto beans rinsed and drained
  * 30 oz canned diced tomatoes with chilies
  * 15 oz canned tomato sacuce
  * 1 1/4 oz chili seasoning
  * 14 oz frozen corn kernels
  * 1 tbsp lime juice
  * 1/2 cup minced cilantro

combine onion, garlic, benas tomatoes, tomato sauce, seasoning in a slow cooker.  cook on high for 2-3 hours or low for 4-6.  add frozen corn during last hour of cooking.  add lime and chilantro just before cooking. 1.33 cups per serving.
