====== Eggs ======

  - Put at room temperature for 15 minutes
  - add cold water to eggs, boil, let boil for 5-7 minutes
  - let soak for 15 minutes, change water to cold water till cool enough to peel
  - peel eggs
  - cover in soy sauce
  - marinate 1 hour

====== pork and eggs ======

  - cook ground pork or chicken, drain oil
  - add fried scallins, nions, bit of brown sugar and some star anise
  - add soy sauce from eggs, garlic and eggs
  - add water to completely cover dish
  - boil a few minutes
