   * 1 8 oz. package of vegetable stir-fry
   * 1 egg
   * Soy sauce
   * 1 tbsp peanut oil

   - Heat peanut oil in skillet.  Add vegetables.
   - Stir-fry until almost done.  In a separate bowl, crack the egg and whisk quickly.
   - Pour egg over whole pan, coating most of the vegetables.
   - Add splashes of soy sauce to taste.
   - Continue cooking until egg is cooked and vegetables are stir-fried to desired texture.
