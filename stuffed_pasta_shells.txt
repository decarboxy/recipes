**Stuffed Jumbo Pasta Shells**	 

  * Marinara meat sauce or other
  * 2 lbs. ricotta or cottage cheese
  * 1/2 lb. Mozzarella cheese, grated
  * 2 eggs
  * 1/2 c. Parmesan, grated
  * 1 tsp. salt
  * 1/4 tsp. pepper
  * 1 tsp parsley

  - Prepare Pasta shells. Bring water to a boil then add pasta shells. Boil pasta for ~15 minutes.
  - Prepare cheese filling. Beat eggs. Stir in cheeses. Mix in seasonings. (I used dill instead of parsley).
  - After pasta shells are cooked. Remove, drain and let cool.
  - When shells are cool enough to handle, stuff shells with cheese filling
  - Preheat oven to 350 deg F
  - In a large casserole dish, layer sauce, meat and pasta shells 
  - Bake in oven for 45 minutes.
