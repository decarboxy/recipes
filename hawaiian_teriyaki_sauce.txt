======Hawaiian Teriyaki Sauce======

For 1 cup approximate final volume. Scale appropriately.

   * 1/3 cup sugar
   * 1/2 soy sauce
   * 1-2 tsp sesame oil
   * 2-3 cloves garlic
  - Dissolve 1/3 cup sugar in 1/2 cup soy sauce and 1-2 tsp sesame oil
  - Add 2-3 cloves grated garlic and 1 tbsp grated ginger.
