   * apple
   * pear
   * olive oil
   * pepper to taste
   * orange juice
   * red wine vinegar


   - Cube apples and pears into small bite-sized chunks.
   - Toss cubed fruit in bowl and drizzle with olive oil.  Sprinkle salt/pepper and toss to coat fruit evenly.
   - Set aside, and in a separate bowl, put 1:1 ratio of orange juice and red wine vinegar.  Mix until color looks even and reminiscent of grape-fruit juice.
   - Pour 1:1 mixture over apples and pears, and toss to coat evenly.
   - Put apple/pear mixture over salad greens.
