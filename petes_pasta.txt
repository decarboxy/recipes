==== Ingredients ====
  * 3 Tb olive oil
  * 6 cloves garlic, minced
  * pine nuts
  * 1 tsp crushed red chlies
  * 1 sweet red pepper, grilled, skinned, chopped (can use the ones in a jar)
  * 4 oz. sundried tomatoes in oil, drain, chop
  * 1 Tb fresh basil, chopped
  * 4 slices bacon, cooked, drained, crumbled (I dice first, fry, drain)
  * Â¼ cup sliced black olives (use kalamata)
  * 12-16 ounces pasta
  * Grated parmesan

==== Directions ====

  - Fry garlic and dried chilies in oil over LOW heat until garlic is lightly browned 5-8 minutes. Add pine nuts and fry briefly.
  - Add the rest of the sauce ingredients. Cook Pasta. Toss with sauce. Sprinkle with parmesan.
