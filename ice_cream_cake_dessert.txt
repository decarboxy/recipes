   * 1/2 gallon vanilla ice cream
   * 2 squares of unsweetened chocolate
   * powdered sugar
   * Salt
   * Vanilla extract
   * peanuts
   * 2 sticks butter
   * 1 tbsp butter
   * 1 box Nabisco Nilla wafers, crushed (food processor)

   - Crush box of Nilla wavers in food processor and pour into large mixing bowl.
   - Melt 2 sticks of butter and add to Nilla wafers.  Mix well.
   - Shape Nilla wafer/butter mix onto bottom of 9"x13" glass pan.
   - Pound peanuts into pieces and press on top of Nilla wafer crust.  Freeze until hardens.
   - Melt 1 tbsp butter, 2 squares of unsweetened chocolate and mix.  Mix in powdered sugar, salt, and vanilla extract and mix.
   - Pour on top of Nilla wafer/peanut crust.  Freeze until hardens or less tacky (30 min.)
   - Set out vanilla ice cream during this time to soften.
   - When chocolate layer has hardened, smooth vanilla ice cream over crust and smooth out surface.
   - Grate chocolate on top of ice cream and set in freezer to harden.  (1 hour)
   - Serve after 1 hour in the freezer.
