   * 1/2 tbsp butter
   * 1 1/4 tsp minced garlic
   * 1/4 cup minced onion
   * salt and pepper to taste
   * 1 14.5 oz. can diced tomatoes with juice
   * 3/4 tsp sugar
   * 1/3 cup heavy cream

   - Melt butter in sauce pan over medium heat.  Stir in garlic/onion and cook until onion turns translucent, 2 min.
   - Pour in diced tomatoes and sugar.
   - Increase heat to medium high and bring to simmer.
   - Reduce heat to medium low and continue simmering for 10 min and stir.
   - Stir in cream and season with salt and pepper.
   - Simmer for an additional 3 minutes.
   - Add 1:1 cornstarch/water mixture to thicken sauce if needed.
   - Serve over pasta.
