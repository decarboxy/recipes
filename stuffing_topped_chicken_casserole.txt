   * 2 chicken breasts
   * 1 can cream of mushroom soup
   * 1 box cornbread stuffing
   * 1/2 cup shredded cheese

   - Season chicken breasts with salt/pepper and cook fully (saute or grill)
   - Make cornbread stuffing following box instructions.
   - In small square glass dish, layer fully cooked chicken, 1/2 can of mushroom soup, cheese, stuffing, and rest of mushroom soup.
   - Cook in oven at 350 F until top is browned.
