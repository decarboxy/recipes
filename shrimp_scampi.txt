   * 1 lb. shrimp
   * 3-4 cloves garlic
   * 4 green onions
   * 1/2 cup white wine
   * 1/2 cup butter
   * 2 tbsp lemon
   * 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
   * pepper, to taste

   - Cook the angel hair pasta as according to the package.
   - Peel and devein shrimp
   - Melt butter in skillet.  When melted, add chopped garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
   - Add shrimp and cook for about 3 minutes, until the shrimp turns pink, but not fully cooked.
   - Add white wine and let shrimp simmer and finish cooking fully in butter/wine mixture.
   - Stir in green onions, let the mixture warm while the green onion gets incorporated.
   - In a big bowl, add the angel hair pasta and butter/wine/shrimp mixture.  Toss until fully coated.
   - Splash with lemon juice and coat with Parmesan cheese and pepper.
