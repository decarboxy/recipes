   * 2 ripe avocados
   * 1/2 chopped onion
   * 1 chopped big garden tomato
   * 2-3 cloves garlic (to taste)
   * 2-3 tbsp lime juice (to taste)
   * 1/2 tsp chili powder (to taste)
   * dash of salt
   * 1/4 cup coarsely chopped cilantro

   - Half and core avocados.  Slice into cubes and scoop out of shell.
   - Mash avocados and when mashed, add in lime juice.
   - Add in garlic, tomatoes, and onion.  Mix well.
   - Add in chili powder and dash of salt to taste.
   - Mix in chopped cilantro.
   - Serves 2-4 (makes roughly 2 cups of dip)
