====== Crispy Onion Rings ======

4 PointsPlus points per 8 ring serving.  makes 4 servings. 

  * 3 sprays cooking spray
  * 1 cup conflake crumbs
  * 1  egg white
  * 1/3 cup low fat milk
  * 1/4 cup flour
  * 3/4 tsp onion powder
  * 1/2 stp table salt
  * 1/8 tsp cayenne pepper
  * 1 large yellow onion seperated into 1/4" thick rings

preheat oven to 450, coat a large rimmed baking sheet with cooking spray.  

Spread cornflake crumbs on a large plate. in a shallow bowl or pie plate, whisk togeher egg white, milk, flour, onion powder, salt,and pepper.

In patches, dip onion rings in egg mixture and dredge in cornflake crumbes, turning to coat both sides.  Place on baking sheet.

Coat onion rings with with cooking spray and bake, turning once, till golden brown  (12 minutes).  sprinkle with salt and pepper before serving.
