   * 4 small red potatoes
   * 2 small onions
   * 2 cloves garlic
   * cumin
   * garlic powder
   * salt/pepper
   * olive oil

   - Cube potatoes.
   - Chop up 2 cloves of garlic and slice 1 onion into long strips.
   - Put potatoes and garlic in a bowl, drizzle with olive oil.  Sprinkle salt, pepper, cumin, and garlic powder over mixture and toss to coat evenly.
   - Saute onions in skillet with olive oil.  Cook until translucent (about 3-5 min)
   - Add in potato/garlic mixture, and stir evenly.  Put top on skillet and let steam for 3-5 min.  Stir mixture.
   - Alternate between steaming and stirring mixture until potatoes are soft and onions are carmelized. (About 20 minutes on skillet overall)
