   * 3/4 cup Bread crumbs
   * 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese
   * 1/4 - 1/2 cup (only need as much to bind and reach a strong goopy consistency) Mayonnaise
   * 2 tbsp (to taste, can add more) Lemon
   * 1 tsp Dill weed
   * Pepper to taste
   * 4 pieces of flounder

   - Mix in bread crumbs and parmesan cheese in bowl.  Add mayonnaise until reaches consistency of chicken salad.  Stir in lemon and pepper to taste.
   - On a cooking sheet, coat evenly with cooking spray and place flounder pieces.  Broil for 2 minutes on each side.
   - Place mayonnaise mixture on top of flounder and broil until browned.
